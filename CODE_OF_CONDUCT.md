# Code Of Conduct

Flower contributors are expected to keep project spaces respectful,
practical, and focused on the work.

Good participation includes:

- Being direct without being hostile.
- Assuming good intent while still naming technical risks clearly.
- Respecting maintainers' time and review scope.
- Keeping disagreements about code, design, or process rather than people.

Unacceptable behavior includes harassment, threats, personal attacks, repeated
derailing, or publishing private information without permission.

Maintainers may remove comments, close discussions, or block participation when
behavior makes the project unsafe or unproductive.

Report conduct concerns through the maintainer contact listed in `pom.xml`.
